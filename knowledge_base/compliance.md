Program Guarantee (Freedom Debt Relief)

What it is: A refund guarantee. If the total amount a client paid for settlements (including fees) exceeded the amount of debt the client enrolled, FDR will refund the difference out of the fees collected (up to 100%) .



This is NOT a savings guarantee. It is a refund promise.





When it applies: After the client exits the program (graduates or leaves).



What voids the guarantee (for a specific debt):



Continuing to charge on an enrolled account.



Taking advances or draws on an enrolled debt.



Removing an account from the program (guarantee no longer applies to that specific account).



Cancellation Policy (From Agreement Walkthrough)



3-Day Cancellation: Clients may cancel the Debt Resolution Agreement without penalty or obligation at any time before midnight of the third business day after signing.





Refund: If canceled within the 3-day period, any money paid will be refunded within 7 days.





Later Termination: Clients may terminate at any later time, but fees paid may not be refunded.
