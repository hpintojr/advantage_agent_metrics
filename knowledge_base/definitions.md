Business Logic \& Metric DefinitionsThis file defines all business rules, metric formulas, and performance standards. It is compiled from Advantage First Production Requirements.docx and Mindset of Execution.docx.Report Headers \& DefinitionsThis section defines the primary metrics for agent performance reports.Agents Full Name: (Agent\\\_Name) Agent's full name from agents.md.Cleared\\\_Debt\\\_Load\\\_MTD: (Cleared\\\_Debt\\\_Load) The total dollar amount of enrolled debt associated with deals that have a "Cleared" first draft status.Total\\\_Enrolled\\\_Debt\\\_MTD: (Total\\\_Enrolled\\\_Debt) The total dollar amount of all new enrolled debt.Closes\\\_MTD: (Enrollments) The total number of new clients enrolled.Avg\\\_Debt\\\_Amount\\\_MTD: (Average\\\_Enrolled\\\_Debt) The average debt amount per enrollment.Total\\\_Calls\\\_MTD: (Total\\\_Conversations) The sum of all inbound and outbound calls with a talk duration > 0.Total\\\_Inbound\\\_MTD: (Inbound\\\_Conversations) Total inbound calls with a talk duration > 0.Total\\\_Outbound\\\_MTD: (Outbound\\\_Conversations) Total outbound calls with a talk duration > 0.Avg\\\_Daily\\\_Outbound\\\_MTD: (Avg\\\_Daily\\\_Outbound\\\_Calls) The average number of outbound conversations per working day.Total\\\_New\\\_Leads\\\_MTD: (Data from CRM) The total number of new leads assigned to the agent.Total\\\_Credit\\\_Pulls\\\_MTD: (Data from CRM) The total number of leads where the agent pulled credit.Total\\\_Submitted\\\_To\\\_Freedom\\\_MTD: (Data from CRM) Total applications submitted to Freedom (or other partners).Cancellations\\\_MTD: (Rescissions) The total number of enrolled accounts that were rescinded or cancelled.Cancellation\\\_Rate\\\_MTD: (Rescission\\\_Rate) Formula: (Cancellations\\\_MTD / Closes\\\_MTD) \\\* 100.Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)These are the core calculated rates based on the report headers.Rescission Rate: (Total Rescissions / Total Enrollments) \\\* 100First Draft Rate: (Total First Drafts / Total Enrollments) \\\* 100Conv per Dial: (Outbound Conversations / Total Dials) \\\* 100Enr per Conv: (Total Enrollments / Total Conversations) \\\* 100Cancellation Ratio Standard: Max 12%. Agents trending higher will be placed in a training review program.Agent Production Requirements (90-Day)Baseline Production: Agents must enroll no less than $650,000 in debt OR a minimum of 18 cleared deals over a rolling 90-day lookback period.PIP: Agents not meeting these standards will be placed on a 30-day Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)."Mindset of Execution" StandardsThese are the required performance percentages for all agents.85% of all payments must be semi-monthly or bi-weekly.90% of all 1st payments required within 3-5 days.90% of all eligible debt must be enrolled in the program.95% of all drafts must be enrolled within the same month as the enrollment (27th to the 27th).100% of enrolled clients to be warm transferred to FDR within the same day.100% of enrolled clients to receive next-day temperature call and draft call (MUST BE NOTED IN FILE).100% of the time, ask for reviews after enrollments.40 (minimum) outbound calls per day.Hybrid / Work-From-Home PolicyAgents within a 45-minute commute are required to work in-person Monday-Friday.WFH on Friday, Saturday, or Sunday is granted only if the agent meets:$850,000 cleared debt80% or higher first-pay rate(Measured over a rolling 90-day period)
